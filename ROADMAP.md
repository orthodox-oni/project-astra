# 🚀 Project Astra Roadmap

> "A living portfolio documenting the journey of a student becoming a software engineer."

---

# Vision

Project Astra is not just a portfolio.

It is a cinematic experience that documents my growth as a developer, learner, and future software engineer.

The goal is to build a website that makes visitors wonder:

> "Wait... a high school student built this?"

Not through fake achievements,
but through thoughtful design, clean code, and attention to detail.

---

# Core Principles

- Build honestly.
- Learn every day.
- Quality over speed.
- Leave evidence of growth.
- Understand before copying.
- Every version should be better than the previous one.
